How we can help mom

Matchmaking

Our volunteers and professionals are here to give you a helping hand with homework and e learning in a memorable way. What about a bit of fun after it? We swear nothing can beat the good old Little Red read by one of our members. Still need some good laughter for the end of the day? We guarantee you our magicians will make you feel like a child again.

Let's get together

You don't have to be a Zen Buddhist to find peace. And if you are calmer, you child will be too. We are happy to help you be in the present, and find silence, acceptance. All of your emotions are valid here. Just like out healers. See you on our free weekly yoga class, women circle, or group meditation.

Edutainment

Struggling with homework, extra classes, and your child has left too much energies, showing no signs of motivation? No worries, you can choose from games like Treasure Hunt, Lead your Hero narrative storyteller, or our quizzes designed on how much time your child can be online. He can even get cool badges like this:) We have thought of you as well. All you have to do is decide which experience you would like to start with.
